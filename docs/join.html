3D Artist

Game Designer

 

Game Designers (a.k.a. Game Engineers/Architects) are the people who create the game before it exists. Involving complex, and at times, technical material, the ideal candidate is someone who is highly motivated, able to multitask, and who is able to research topics effectively.

You know how to work in a team. You have good communication skills, and a creative and inventive outlook.

Concept Artist

 

As a concept artist you're able to effectively visualize ideas in both rudimentary as detailed sketches. You love the look of science fiction and know how to create a distinct look and feel by using only pen and paper.

Knowing how to create digital artwork by using Photoshop, The Gimp, Corel Painter or similar is highly recommended.
