My pet hates
As I have been playing XCOM Enemy Unknown, I would just like to ask why so many "features" or bugs were in it.

My pet hates:

1) Why 80 items? Just WHY?!
2) You can fire THROUGH UFO walls now, reaaaaally?
3) How come that floater can survive a heavy plasma blast at point blank range so well it simply kills the soldier who fires?
4) Why bother with anything but autofire? No point is there?
5) Why are the civilians so STUPID?! Stop running round in circles, and get the hell out of there....
6) .... I didn't say run straight onto the craft now, did I? How am I supposed to get the rest of the platoon out?
7) An entire craft of sarges and capts... erm.. those ranks sound a little screwy..
8) Why on earth would the commander of all XCOM forces get off the plane first? Asking for it if you ask me...
9) There's a research tree, not pick the route you want in order to get there...
10) How come it takes so long to heal your soldiers? Do they take a month's paid vacation as well as being treated?
11) Same goes for aircraft... what are they waiting for? Christmas?!


Probably more, will think of some more later.
