My thoughts on Aftermath
 
I went out yesterday and bought UFO Aftermath for only $10 and I have been doing some 'research' since.

By using accelerated game play(cheats), I have come to the conclusions that it is a very impressive game.

Likes:
-RPG elements in squads
-Ease of use in BattleScape
-Looks much better than the original UFO series
-Interesting storyline
-Realtimeish Battlescape

Dislikes:
-Bases cannot be assigned individual research/development projects
-Only one squad can be deployed at a time
-Only one type of attack craft
-Lack of variety in aliens
-Bases are a defined type, cannot be hybrid(e.g. a researching military base)
