Re: What's the current status and team direction?
 
Hi Yavor,

We're currently building up a team that will build milestone 1. This first milestone will consist of the core game engine and logic that is put to use in a model viewer. The model viewer creation will offer us the possibility to easily test and evaluate our work in a shorter development period. Development officially starts on 15 november.

Furthermore in this milestone, game engineers and architects will build the foundations for Milestone 2. The goal of that milestone can be seen once you've become a member. We do not want to spoil any surprises ;)

The general goals with requirements have been made 1 year in advance. This means that we have a deadline with fixed goals to reach within one year.

We are a team with professional endeavours. The Quasar community rules strictly forbid any form of discrimination, racism, vulgar, ... behaviour. Any team- or forummembers violating this agreement get a warning. Whenever that warning doesn't stop the person from violating the rules, he/she gets banned.

I hope this answers your question. If you have any other questions, feel free to ask them by creating a new thread in this subforum.
