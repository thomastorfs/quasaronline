Cool Movie
 
There's a lot of really nice stuff at this web site, but I wanted to draw particular attention to this video. It was made entirely by one person (except for the music) over a ten month period. This animation is amazongly professional in design and execution. The lighting is superb, the characters are very well developed, and the animation timing of movement is amazingly well done. I find this video to be quite inspiring in terms of what a highly motivated indivisual can accomplish without the need for big studio resources or funding. If somebody had shown me this video and said it was made by a full team at Pixar, I wouldn't have questioned it.
